Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 24, 2023

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit or use Liberia Connect. We are committed to protecting your data, especially concerning Mobile Money transactions and location services, while ensuring our platform remains lightweight and accessible for all users across Liberia.

1 Information We Collect

When you visit the Site, we automatically collect certain information about your device, including information about your web browser, IP address, time zone, and some of the cookies that are installed on your device. We prioritize collecting minimal data to ensure the app loads quickly on slower connections.

Additionally, as you browse the Site, we collect information about the individual web pages or products that you view, what websites or search terms referred you to the Site, and information about how you interact with the Site.

2 Mobile Money & Transactions

Liberia Connect integrates with local Mobile Money providers (such as MTN Mobile Money and Orange Money) to facilitate secure payments between clients and service providers.

  • We do not store your PIN. Your Mobile Money PIN is never saved on our servers.
  • Transaction Confirmation: We only receive confirmation of successful payments to release funds or confirm bookings.
  • Provider Verification: Providers receiving payments must verify their identity using a valid Liberian ID card linked to their Mobile Money account.

3 How We Use Your Data

We use the Order Information that we collect generally to fulfill any orders placed through the Site (including processing your payment information, arranging for services, and providing you with invoices and/or order confirmations).

Additionally, we use this Order Information to:

chat

Communicate with you

Sending booking confirmations and service updates via SMS or WhatsApp.

shield

Screen for risk

Checking orders for potential risk or fraud, specifically related to cash-on-delivery tasks.

4 Location Information

As a local service marketplace, connecting you with nearby providers requires general location data. We may request access to your device's geolocation to show you relevant tasks or providers in your vicinity (e.g., Monrovia, Paynesville, Buchanan).

Note: You can use the app without precise GPS by manually selecting your city or neighborhood to save battery and data.

Questions about your privacy?

For more information about our privacy practices, if you have questions, or if you would like to make a complaint, please contact us.
