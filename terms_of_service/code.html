3 Mobile Money Payments

All transactions on Liberia Connect are processed via Lonestar Cell MTN Mobile Money or Orange Money. We do not store credit card information.

warning Important: Ensure you have sufficient funds in your mobile wallet before booking a service. Escrow services hold funds until task completion is verified.

We minimize image downloads and use compressed formats to respect your data plan limits.
